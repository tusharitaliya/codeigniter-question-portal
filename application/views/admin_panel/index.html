ABOUT US

Mya Design Consultants is a consulting firm specialized in Interior Design and Architecture. It was founded by architect and interior designer May Bdour.

We design beautiful functional spaces that are a joy to live in. We take time to listen to you and understand your needs and translate all your wishes into designs.

Going into the smallest details without overlooking the great picture we simply love what we do and are passionate about designing and building our projects. We cover a client/ project list of well established companies and household names from around the world. Our tight knit design team concentrates on superb design solutions tailored for each individual project with great attention to detail and a high level of commitment.
